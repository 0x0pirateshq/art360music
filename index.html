WELCOME TO ART360

At Art360, we bring your musical aspirations to life through expert instruction and state-of-the-art studio services. Whether you're eager to learn a new instrument, hone your production skills, or record your latest masterpiece, we have the resources and expertise to support your journey.

Services Offered.

Guitar Lessons

From the basics of strumming to advanced solos, our guitar lessons cater to all skill levels. Learn various styles including rock, blues, jazz, and classical.

Piano Lessons

Whether you aspire to perform classical masterpieces or contemporary hits, our piano courses are designed to help you achieve your goals.

Drum Lessons

Discover the rhythm within you. Our drum lessons cover a range of styles from rock and jazz to classical percussion technique.

Music Production

Dive into the world of music production with our comprehensive courses. Learn everything from recording and mixing to mastering and producing your own tracks.

Vocals

Unlock your vocal potential with our expert training in both classical and western singing techniques. Our personalized approach ensures that you develop a strong and versatile voice.

Online Classes

Distance is no barrier to learning at Art360. Our online classes provide high-quality music education wherever you are. With live, interactive lessons and a range of digital resources, you can achieve your musical goals without leaving your home.

Whether you're learning guitar, piano, drums, vocals, or music production, our dedicated instructors are here to guide you every step of the way. Certified Courses

Our curriculum is structured to provide a comprehensive musical education, from beginner to advanced levels.
